I am Calypso,
And nobody can stay
On this island
Made for one;
 
So the warning goes.
 
I have wondered
What I did before
To earn this prison,
Struggled to
Remember past lives,
Where I must have done
Terrible things
To deserve this desert
That floats in deseret
 
An island of
Waiting,
Finding,
Falling,
And being left behind.
 
Yes,
They always say
They love me,
But none of them stay.
 
Just stay!
I entreat,
But they don’t;
They can’t.
 
It is my inheritance.
I cannot escape it.
 
You may try to
Cheat fate,
You may drive
Destiny to its knees,
But you will leave me,
Willingly,
After a time.
There is no contest.
 
You certainly,
Surely will depart,
As each and every did before,
And burn a notch
Into my heart
As you pass
Under my pine trees,
The only thing
I am not allergic to,
The only beings
Who will leave with me,
And not before,
My only allies in this war.
